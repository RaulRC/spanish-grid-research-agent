# Example research questions

Questions a user might ask the agent, ordered roughly from simpler to more
complex multi-step investigations.

## Price fundamentals

- *"Why did Spanish day-ahead prices spike on [specific date]?"*
- *"How much of the price on [date] was driven by gas CCGT vs renewable inframarginal pricing?"*
- *"Compare average hourly prices for the last week with the same week last year."*
- *"What was the price spread between peak and off-peak hours yesterday?"*

## Renewables & grid

- *"On [date], solar PV hit X GW — how did that depress midday prices?"*
- *"Was there wind curtailment on [date]? Check generation vs. demand + exports."*
- *"How does CO₂ intensity correlate with the wind+solar share over the last month?"*
- *"What share of generation came from renewables in [month]?"*

## PVPC vs wholesale

- *"When in the last 30 days did PVPC diverge most from the day-ahead price, and why?"*
- *"What's the PVPC 2.0TD spread vs. the day-ahead price by hour-of-day this month?"*

## Weather-driven

- *"A cold front hit Madrid on [date] — did demand spike and prices follow?"*
- *"How did wind generation evolve during storm [name] compared to the prior week?"*
- *"How did temperatures in Seville correlate with demand peaks last summer?"*

## Cross-border

- *"Was Spain exporting to France on [date] during their nuclear outages?"*
- *"How did net imports correlate with Spanish prices this quarter?"*

## Multi-step investigations

- *"Walk me through what happened on [date]: demand, generation mix, prices, cross-border flows, and the weather at key stations."*
- *"Why was the last week of [month] unusual? Check prices, renewables output, and any interconnector issues."*
